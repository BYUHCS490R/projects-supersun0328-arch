About Me

Hi, I’m David Sun, a digital artist currently studying visual art. I focus on illustration and character design, and I enjoy exploring different styles and compositions in my work.

Most of my work is created digitally using tools like Clip Studio Paint. I am currently learning more about shading, lighting, and composition to improve my overall artwork quality.
