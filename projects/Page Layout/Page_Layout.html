What is Sun Moon lake?

Sun Moon Lake is one of the most famous natural places in Taiwan. It is located in Nantou County and many people visit it every year. The lake is known for its peaceful scenery, mountains, and beautiful sunrise views.

The name “Sun Moon Lake” comes from the shape of the lake. One side looks round like the sun, while the other side looks like a crescent moon. Because of this unique shape and the calm atmosphere, it has become a popular place for travel and photography.
