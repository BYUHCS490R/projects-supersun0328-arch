About Rhythm Games

Rhythm games are a genre of video games that challenge players to follow musical patterns and timing. Players interact with the game by tapping, clicking, or moving in sync with the music. These games are popular because they combine music, skill, and visual design. Many rhythm games also have strong communities that create custom content or share scores.
