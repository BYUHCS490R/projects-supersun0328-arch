My Hero - Moroni

Moroni from the Book of Mormon is my hero. He was a strong and faithful leader who protected his people. I admire him because he showed courage, obedience, and responsibility.

Background

Moroni was a hero of the Nephite army. He lived during a time of war and helped defend his people from their enemies.

Role
Captain of the Nephite army and a strong leader during wartime.
Known for
Creating the Title of Liberty to remind people to defend their freedom, faith, and families.
Character traits
Brave, responsible, obedient, and willing to act when others were afraid.
