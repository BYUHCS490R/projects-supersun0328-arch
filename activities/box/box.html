Box Model

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

  • Block tags start on a new line and take up the full width available
  • Inline tags do not start on a new line and only take up as much space as their content needs

What is the margin, border, and padding?

  • Margin is the space outside the element.
  • Border is the line surrounding the element
  • Padding is the space between the content and the border.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

  • px
  • %
  • em
  • rem
  • vh
  • vw

When would you use a figure tag?

  • to group media content (like an image, chart, or diagram)
